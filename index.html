Hotel room vs. home-style stay

See the difference

Hotel rooms are wonderful. But for families, groups and longer trips, a home changes everything.

Traditional hotel room

  • Limited square footage
  • One primary sleeping area
  • Little separate living space
  • Often no kitchen
  • Multiple rooms needed for groups
vs

Home, villa or residence

  • Multiple bedrooms
  • Separate living & dining areas
  • Dramatically more space
  • Kitchens & laundry often available
  • Everyone together, in comfort
